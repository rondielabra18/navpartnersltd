QUESTION # 1: What is required to accept a Specified Skilled Worker?

The accepting company must conclude an employment contract, provide appropriate working conditions, and prepare a support plan. By working with a Registered Support Organization, the process becomes smoother and compliant with regulations.

02

QUESTION # 2: How long does the hiring process take?

• Domestic candidates: about 1–2 months
• Overseas candidates: about 3–6 months

The timeline depends on the country, industry, and documentation

03

QUESTION # 3: What level of Japanese language ability is required?

In principle, workers must have JLPT N4 level or equivalent Japanese ability. Some industries may require higher proficiency.

04

QUESTION # 4: What services does a Registered Support Organization provide?

We provide:

  • Pre-arrival orientation
  • Airport pick-up
  • Housing setup
  • Administrative procedures
  • Regular interviews
  • Japanese language support
  • Emergency assistance
05

QUESTION # 5: How much does the service cost?

Costs vary depending on the industry, number of workers, and support level. We offer a free consultation and provide a customized quotation.
