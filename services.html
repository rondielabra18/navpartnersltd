Common Issues and Our Solutions

Manners Violation / Noise

Issue: Rules are not fully understood due to language barriers.


Solution:

Hands-on Training such as garbage separation and cleaning. Learning by doing.

Overwhelming Complaint Handling

Issue: Company Staff becomes exhausted handling complaints


Solution:

We personally greet neighbors and ask them to contact us directly, preventing issues from escalating to the company.

Loneliness and Isolation

Issue: Workers struggle to adapt and feel isolated.


Solution:

Native-language counseling and accompaniment to community activities for emotional support.
